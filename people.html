The staff are the business and here they are

Ben Swindell

Ben qualified in 1990 gaining the student of the year class prize in each of the qualifying years, progressing to study Advanced courses in Conservation technology and Prosthodontics, earning distinction for each qualification. He subsequently taught BTEC students at the Castle College in Nottingham relinquishing this role when he became a partner in Crown Ceramics. He now has Specialist Lecturer status in the College course team.

In 2007/8 Ben studied for the Dental Technology BSc.(Hons.) at DeMontfort University, Leicester and graduated with first class honours.

Ben is interested in all aspects of Crown and Bridge work particularly implantology and cosmetic dentistry.

He is married to Jenny and has two sons.

Andy Platts

Andrew qualified in 1992 gaining student of the year recognition and subsequently progressing to study Advanced level Conservation technology, passing with distinction.

Andrew became a partner in the business in 1999 and continues to keenly pursue his interest in ceramics and implantology.

He is married to Rachel and has two daughters.
