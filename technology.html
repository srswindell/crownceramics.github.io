Technology

We have in house laser welding capability, this greatly enhances our production methods. Laser welding techniques cause limited heat generation avoiding distortion. Superior accuracy and bio-compatibility (no intermediary dissimilar solder alloy). Repairs, adjustment and additions can be effected upon the master cast reducing the possibility of error. The team have undertaken specialist training at the prestigious Academia Dental, the BEGO facility in Bremen, Germany.

We have, in house a state of the art parallelometer and are experienced in precision attachment work along with staff who have been trained at the C & M facility in Biel/Bienne, Switzerland
