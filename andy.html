Andrew Platts RDT LBIDST
Licentiate of BDIST
Registered Member DTA
GDC Reg no. 124384

Andrew qualified in 1992 gaining student of the year recognition and subsequently progressing to study Advanced level Conservation technology, passing with distinction.

Andrew became a partner in the business in 1999 and continues to keenly pursue his interest in ceramics and implantology.

He is married to Rachel and has two daughters.
