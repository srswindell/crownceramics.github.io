About Crown Ceramics

The laboratory was established in 1986 by Ralph Swindell and Colin Wylie, former directors of O`Hara Dental Laboratory Ltd. in Nottingham. Crown Ceramics moved in to their current custom designed premises in 1992. Ben Swindell and Andrew Platts became junior partners in 1998 and took over ownership the following year.

Ben and Andrew have continued the ethos of the laboratory in investing in new technology and equipment and consistently updating their skill and knowledge base to provide an effective, high quality and personal service.

Crown Ceramics are signed up to the DLA code of practice
