Quality

To demonstrate our commitment we have gained DAMAS registration No. 980021 and MDD registered No.CA000049

Crown Ceramics provides high quality work and an excellent service to our clients and membership of DAMAS shows

  • We meet the requirements of the Medical Devices Directive (MDD) and the Medical Devices Regulations (MDR).
  • We use tried and tested materials which help protect the health of patients.
  • We are geared up to meet the needs of both the NHS and the private dental market.
  • We value the skills of our employees.
  • We care about the service that we provide.

By using a DAMAS registered laboratory you can be confident that we adhere to all UK regulations relating to lab work and the production of dental appliances. Your patients will have the reassurance of excellent quality craftsmanship.

The scheme is supported by a host of dental industry partners:

  • British Dental Association.
  • General Dental Practitioners Association.
  • Dental Protection.
  • Department of Health.
  • AMTAC Certification Services Ltd.
  • British Dental Health Association.
  • British Dental Trades Association.
